About us

Yoga is a light, which once lit will never dim. The better your practice, the brighter your flame.

Classes

In every class you will do appropriate warm ups, yoga postures, yoga breathing (pranayama) deep relaxation (yoga nidra) and meditation (dhyana). Yoga is non competitive and everything you do in class is done to your personal ability. You will not be asked to do anything that isn’t right for you.

Meditation

The meditation we do is based on the yogic style, stilling the body and mind through yoga nidra, pranayama and breath awareness. These sessions are held on a monthly basis on Saturday mornings 11.00 am – 1.00 pm. Suitable for beginners and those with experience.

Yoga Teacher Training

Our course will provide you with a comprehensive, theoretical and hands-on training programme. This will give you well-grounded teaching skills to make you an effective yoga teacher.
